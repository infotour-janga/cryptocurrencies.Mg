Cryptocurrency & Earning

1. What is cryptocurrency ?

Cryptocurrency is an online payment system, in which transactions are virtual and independent of banks or other fiscal control.

2. How does it work ?

Cryptocurrency use decentralized network, it is Blockchain technology which manages all online transactions.

3. What is Blockchain technology ?

Blockchain technology is a distributed and decentralized data storage and transmission technology. It is used as an account book accessible for everyone, the Blockchain is indestructible and unpublished once the data is integrated.

Why should we use cryptocurrency ?

Despite these flaws, such as the unpredictable oscillation of value, the risks of usurpation and scams, and alerts on money laundering, we suggest the use of cryptocurrency on the following points :
- Unpredictable variation of values ​​but increasing trends over time;
- Confidentiality in use and possession, no obligation to reveal real identity;
- Easy online payments and secure transactions under Blockchain.
